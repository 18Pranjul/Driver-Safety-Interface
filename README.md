# Driver-Safety-Interface

Need

India is the No.1 contributor to global road crash mortality and morbidity figures. Every hour, around 16 lives are lost to road crashes in India. In the last decade alone, India lost 1.3 million people to road crashes and another 5.3 million were disabled for life, and yet, drivers fail to give up risky habits. Hence, this led to our idea of extending this project.
